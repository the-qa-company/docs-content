Here are some links that may help you getting started with QAnswer.

## Playgrounds
Playgrounds are interactive environments where you can try out QAnswer and explore its capabilities.

:::tip
No account is required to use the Playgrounds.
:::

We offer four different playgrounds, each designed to let you experiment with different types of data:

* [Text Playground](/playgrounds/text): Use text data as input.
* [Knowledge Graph Playground](/playgrounds/graph/description): Use RDF data as input.
* [PDF Playground](/playgrounds/document): Use a PDF file as input.
* [Chatbot Playground](/playgrounds/chatbot): Have a conversation with our chatbot.

In the playgrounds, you can ask questions based on your data, make modifications, and see how QAnswer responds to your changes!
