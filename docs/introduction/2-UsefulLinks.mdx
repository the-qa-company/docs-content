Here are some links that may help you getting started with QAnswer.

## Playgrounds
Playgrounds are places you can try out QAnswer and learn how to use it.

As input you have either rdf data or text data.
You can ask question on it, modify your data and see how QAnswer reacts to your changes. 

:::info
You don't need an account to have fun with the Playgrounds, try them out here:

:::caution
What you write in the playgrounds will not be saved.
:::
