Now you are ready to upload your first dataset. On your [QA Systems page](https://app.qanswer.ai/qa-systems) click on *Add dataset*.

<Image src='/img/newUI/tuto-ui-text/qa-systems-empty.png' alt='Home'/>

You can chose what type of QA system you want to create, based on graph or on text.

Let's add a free-text dataset.


<Image src='/img/newUI/wizard.png' alt='Wizard'/>


Now you can name your QA-System, customize its parameters and upload your files.
