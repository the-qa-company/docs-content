## Creation
Pick the name and language of your QA System, upload your files (click or drag and drop on the dedicated area), you can click on:
* <kbd>Settings</kbd> to personnalize them (default parameters are good for general use)
* <kbd>Finish</kbd> to create your QA System (and use default parameters)


<Image src='/img/screenshots/tutorial-ui/document-based/creation-init.png' alt='Creation'/>

:::info
You can also add an icon for your QA system. It will be displayed in the list of QA systems.
:::

## Settings (optional)

Then you can customize your QA System parameters if needed (default parameters are good for general use).

<Image src='/img/screenshots/tutorial-ui/document-based/creation-settings.png' alt='Settings'/>

After clicking on the <kbd>Finish</kbd> button, you will be redirected to the home page and you
will have your newly built QA system appearing in the list.
