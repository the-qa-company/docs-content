You can access the <kbd>Expose</kbd> section by clicking on the on the top bar after choosing the AI Assistant you want to work with.
In the Expose section you can choose the website where you want your AI Assitant to be available. The integration can take 3 forms :
chatbot, search interface or chat interface.
