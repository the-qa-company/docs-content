<p>
    It's time to build your first QA system over textual documents! In the panel <a href='https://app.qanswer.ai/qa-systems'>QA Systems</a>, click on
    <span style={{ whiteSpace: 'nowrap' }}>
        <img src='/img/icons/add_circle.svg' style={{ width: '30px', padding: '0 5px', verticalAlign: 'middle' }}/>
        <b style={{ verticalAlign: 'middle', color: '#666666', marginRight: '10px' }}>Add</b>
    </span>
    button.
</p>

<Image src='/img/screenshots/qa-systems-empty.png' alt='Home'/>

Here we are adding a free text dataset! So we will select the <b>Document based</b> option.

<Image src='/img/screenshots/qa-systems-empty-add-btn.png' alt='Add system'/>

You will be redirected to the creation page.

Pick the name and language of your QA System, upload your files (click or drag and drop on the dedicated area) and click on the <kbd>Next</kbd> button.


<Image src='/img/screenshots/tuto-ui-text/creation-init.png' alt='Creation'/>

:::info
You can also add an icon for your QA system. It will be displayed in the list of QA systems.
:::

Then you can customize your QA System parameters if needed (default parameters are good for general use).

<Image src='/img/screenshots/tuto-ui-text/creation-settings.png' alt='Settings'/>

After clicking on the <kbd>Finish</kbd> button, you will be redirected to the home page and you
will have your newly built QA system appearing in the list.
