<Image src='/img/screenshots/wizard.png' alt='Wizard'/>

Pick the name of the QA system, customize its parameters if needed (default
parameters are good for general use), and then upload your documents to finish the
creation of your QA system.

After clicking on the <kbd>Finish</kbd> button, you will be redirected to the home page and you
will have your newly built QA system appearing in the list.
