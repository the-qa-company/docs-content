It's possible to edit your document collection on the **Editing** page. You can access the **Editing** page using the button in the status bar (on top of your screen) or in the **QA Systems** page by clicking on the QA system you are interested in and then the *Edit* button.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', flexDirection: 'column', gap: '10px', marginBottom: '20px' }}>
    <img src='/img/screenshots/tuto-ui-text/status-bar.png' alt='training button' width= '60%'/>
    <img src='/img/screenshots/tuto-ui-text/qa-system-edit-btn.png' alt='training button' width= '60%'/>
</div>

On this page you can see all documents that are in your collection, you can also see their content and delete some parts of it. You can also add new files to your collection.

Let's add Lisa Simpson to our collection by using the **Add file** button. And then let's see the content of the document by clicking on the **View file** button. You can also edit the document, by clicking on the **Edit file** button. You can download the file here:
