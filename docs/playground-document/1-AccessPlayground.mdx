## Access from your account

:::info
If you don't have an account yet, let's create one to get started! [see tutorial](/introduction/register-login)
:::

Once your account is created, you can login and start discovering the platform!

<div style={{ display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'center', marginBottom: '20px' }}>
    <Link
        to='https://app.qanswer.ai'
        className="button button--primary button--lg"
    >
        Open QAnswer!
    </Link>
</div>

When you are logged in, you will enter in the <b>QA Systems</b> page:

<Image src='/img/screenshots/qa-systems-empty.png' alt='QA Systems'/>

Click <i>New Playground</i> and then choose the **Document** option.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '10px', marginBottom: '20px', justifyContent: 'center' }}>
    <img src='/img/screenshots/navbar-open-playground-text.png' alt='nav bar' style={{ height: '300px' }}/>
    <img src='/img/screenshots/navbar-closed-playground.png' alt='nav bar closed' style={{ height: '300px' }}/>
</div>

You will get here:

<Image src='/img/screenshots/playground-document/playground-document.png' alt='playground'/>

You can click or drag and drop your file to the dedicated area (on the left side of the page).

You can use the <i>Clear</i> button to remove the file.

:::caution
This is a playground so **it will not be saved**.
:::
